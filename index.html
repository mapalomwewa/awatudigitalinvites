How it works

Three steps to beautiful

We handle the design. You share the link. Your guests RSVP in seconds.

01
Choose a package

Select Basic (K300), Standard (K650), or Custom (K1,800). Pay via Airtel Money, MTN, bank transfer, or cash in Lusaka.

02
Share your details

Send us your names, date, venue, and photos via WhatsApp. No complicated forms — we tailor every invitation personally.

03
Send your link

We deliver your invitation link within 24 hours. Share on WhatsApp and track every RSVP live from your personal dashboard.

Awatu.
Digital Invitations · Lusaka, Zambia
Awatu. is a digital invitation studio crafting beautiful, personalised invitations for weddings, graduations, corporate events, and every moment worth celebrating.

We combine thoughtful design with technology — making it effortless for our clients to share their most important moments with elegance. From concept to delivery, every invitation is handled with care.

Awatu is a proud subdivision of the Edge Group of Companies, a Lusaka-based portfolio of businesses committed to creative excellence, professional services, and digital innovation across Zambia.
